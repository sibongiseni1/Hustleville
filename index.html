VELAPHI MOTSOENENG

The 22-year-old, Velaphi motsoeneng is a central midfielder, born in Thokoza in the East of Joburg, plays for Tkzee Fc in the SAFA Ekurhuleni Men's Football League.A former Wits Junior player who also went to Turkey in Europe to enhance his abilites and skills as both a player and an individual, he is known as a the 'Chain Puller' because of his ability to take shots from long distance.

TSHEPO MNGOMEZULU

The 23-year-old, Tshepo Mngomezulu is a central midfielder born in Katlehong in the East of Joburg.He is the captain for FBS FC in Hollywood Ekurhuleni Men's Regional football league and led his side to victory in both the 2023 Gold One Champions and the Ekurhuleni regional Men's Nedbank Cup.

TUMELO MOTAUNG

The 22-year-old from Thokoza in the East of Joburg, is a versatile player who can fully fit in most positions like the central defence, right back and winger, he is playing for the NWU Tawana FC side in the North West Dr Kaunda ABC Motsepe League and also a student at North West University.
